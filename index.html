Welcome to Mindr

Mindr is a simple and elegant way to inject calm into our lives. Share how you're feeling and Mindr will provide affirmation and a chance to reflect and meditate so you can be healthier and happier.

How can Mindr help you?

Stress takes a serious toll on the body. It distracts us from getting on and enjoying life. And if you let it get the better of you, it can even make you physically ill.

Mindr helps you manage stress by countering the "flight-or-fight" response that results from stress.Taking even a brief moment to slow down, collect your thoughts and reflect can have immense benefits when your mind is racing.
